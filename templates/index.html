If it is your first time here, start by maintaining your default data through 'update favorites' link. You can maintain the IBAN number, payment term, and contact information so that the application always suggests them to you by default when recording new income, saving you from unnecessary typing

If it is time to record new income or expense, select the corresponding link from the top. You can also enter the record view, which includes links for adding both income and expenses.

On the Record View, you can review your income and expenses and open the invoice in a new browser window, allowing you to print it as a PDF.

You can review your account information, i.e. business id and business name through "Account" link. You are able to update those but please note that it will affect to your accounting history as well. It might be that you want to make a small change to your business name that does not matter from accounting history point of view or correct a type in your business id. However, if you are starting a new business, please create a new user account for that (by logging out and registering with new information).

Accounting is not a favorite activity for many. Single record accounting is already quite a word monster as a term. Many of us would be happier as small business operators without invoices and income statements.

Single record accounting app lightens the load of accounting, leaving you with more time for the "real work." With this application, you can create income and expense lines using an easy form, which immediately updates your accounting view and generates invoice views from the income lines. Before you start, please make sure that single record accounting is sufficient for your business.

In Finland, single record accounting is permitted for businesses that meet at most one of the following conditions:

  • The balance sheet total exceeds 200,000 euros
  • The revenue exceeds 200,000 euros
  • It employs at least 3 persons

If this sounds right to you, then let's get started! To continue, you need to log in or register from the upper right corner.
